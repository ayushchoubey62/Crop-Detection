About Crop Disease Detection App

Application Overview

The Crop Disease Detection App is an innovative tool designed to assist farmers and agricultural enthusiasts in identifying common plant leaf diseases using advanced machine learning models. Our goal is to provide quick, accessible, and preliminary diagnoses to help in timely intervention and better crop management.

Key Features:

  • AI-powered disease detection for various crops.
  • Image upload and live camera capture capabilities (browser dependent).
  • Image enhancement tools for better analysis.
  • Detailed diagnosis reports with confidence scores and treatment suggestions.
  • History tracking of past diagnoses.
  • User feedback system for continuous improvement.
